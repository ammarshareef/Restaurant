About Us

We welcome you to indulge your taste buds with delicacies straight from the kitchen of the Nizams.

We Are BiryaniOverdose.

Celebrating Hyderabad's Traditional Hospitality We truly believe that food is not just a meal. We look at it just the way the Hyderabadi royalty did - as a prized possession, a symbol of pride. We honour this relic of a delicacy with the same grace and heartiness of the Nawabs and we serve it to you the way the Nawabs appreciated it, in a truly Hyderabadi fashion, with Mehman Nawazi. When you eat our biryani, you feel right at home a home that you can keep coming back to, as you will always feel welcome here!
