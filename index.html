Welcome to BiryaniOverdose

Authenticity that has survived centuries! For the last 400 years, biryani has been both Hyderabad's pride and its most cherished attraction. However, this love affair between the two didn't start here. When Mughals first brought Biryani to Hyderabad, the recipe saw a cultural influence of Hyderabad's own unique flavours, resulting in the aromatic and sensory Biryani that we know today. For years, this improved recipe was a closely guarded secret of the Nizam's kitchen because it was considered to be a royal dish. Today, only a few people can bring the richness, the same savoury deliciousness of the original Biryani to the table. And Biryani Overdose is one of them.
